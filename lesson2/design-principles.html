Visual Hierarchy

Wild Waves

wildwaves.com

This Wild Waves amusment park website is a good example of visual hierarchy. You can tell exactly where they want you to go by making the section for Season Passes BIG and bold. They also have the "Sale" announcement larg so that you know that there is a discount on season passes.

Alignment

Point Defiance Zoo & Aquarium

pdza.org

The Point Defiance Zoo shows great allignment with their photo links on this page. Even though the pictures are all different sizes, there are clear borders and everything looks neat and even. I realy like how there is two clear columns between the top and bottom picture.

White Space & Clean Design

The Space Needle

spaceneedle.com

For this Space Needle website, I love how it uses the white space of the sky behind the space needle. They didn't fill it full of words or anouncements which makes it look nicer and more appealing to look at. It goes right along with their message, "Clearly Awe-Inspiring." The empty space around the top also makes the design look clean and organized.
