Audience

Our target audience is the consumers. We want to let them know what kind of commerce they can expect, whether they are planning to settle down in our fine city or they are just here for a visit. We also want to connect new and existing businesses in our community

Personas

Hello, my name is Cinder and I am a mechanic from New Beijing. I can fix anything from androids, to port screens, to space crafts. There is no job too small or too big for me. I have been thinking of relocating to Artemisia city on luna and wanted to check out the other local businesses there.

My name is Cress and I am a computer whiz. I have been working for the royal family making sure that they are never without security surveillance and satellite services. Need a bug planted, a Nobleman monitored, or a computer system hacked, then I am your girl. I am Artemisia's top IT tech and ready to serve you.

Scenarios

  1. New business owners can come and take a look at the other businesses in the area. This can help them to get an idea of where they will stand and what other businesses are doing to succeed.
  2. Existing business can come to find support and camaraderie amongst other business owners. They can also find services that they may need for thier own business' success.
  3. Individuals and familys can come to our website when they are thinking of moving to our beautiful lunar city. This will help them to make the decision of whether Artemisia is the right place for them to live.
  4. Current community members are welcome to visit our site to find new businesses and rediscover the old ones. This can help connect both consumers and business owners.
  5. This site can also help tourists who would like to know where to go and what to expect while visiting our beautiful city.
